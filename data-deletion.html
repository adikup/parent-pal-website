Data Deletion Request

How to request deletion of your personal data from Carri

Your Privacy Matters: We respect your right to control your personal data. You can request the deletion of all your personal information from our database at any time.
Important: We can only delete data from our database. For calendar data that was synced to your Google Calendar, you have two options:
  • Option 1: Use the app's "Sign Out & Reset All Data" feature (recommended)
  • Option 2: Manually delete events from your Google Calendar

üìß How to Request Data Deletion

1

Send an Email

Send an email to contact.carri.app@gmail.com with the subject line: "Data Deletion Request - Carri App"

2

Include Required Information

In your email, please include the following information to help us identify your account:

  • Your full name
  • The email address associated with your Carri account
  • Your phone number (if used for account verification)
  • Confirmation that you want to delete all your data
3

Wait for Confirmation

We will process your request within 30 days and send you a confirmation email once your data has been deleted from our database.

4

Handle Calendar Data (If Applicable)

If you had calendar events synced to your Google Calendar, you'll need to handle those separately:

  • Recommended: Open the Carri app and use "Sign Out & Reset All Data" feature
  • Alternative: Manually delete Carri-related events from your Google Calendar
